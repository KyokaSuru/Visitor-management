🏡 Subdivision Visitor Management System (GateKeeper-QR)
A modern, secure, and efficient digital solution designed to streamline and monitor visitor entry within residential subdivisions. By replacing outdated manual logbooks with QR-code-based identification, this application ensures faster gate check-ins, reduces traffic congestion, and enhances overall community safety.

🌟 Key Features
🎫 QR-Code Visitor Passes: Generates unique, time-bound QR codes for guests, delivery riders, and contractors for seamless scanning at the guardhouse.

📲 Resident Portal / Pre-Registration: Allows homeowners to pre-register their guests via the app, instantly generating a QR pass to share with visitors beforehand.

⚡ Instant Gate Check-In/Out: Security guards can quickly scan QR codes using a mobile device or dedicated scanner, instantly logging entry and exit timestamps.

📊 Real-Time Traffic & Occupancy Tracking: Provides subdivision administrators and HOA officials with a live dashboard to monitor who is currently inside the community.

🔔 Guard-to-Resident Alerts: Automatically notifies residents when a guest or delivery rider has successfully passed through the main gate.

🚨 Blacklist & Unauthorized Access Alerts: Instantly flags expired QR codes or restricted individuals at the scanning point.
